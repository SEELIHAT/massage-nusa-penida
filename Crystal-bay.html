Crystal Bay in Nusa Penida is a stunning beach known for its crystal-clear waters and excellent diving and snorkeling opportunities. Located on the west coast of the island, Crystal Bay is famous for its vibrant coral reefs, diverse marine life, and thefull zero chance to encounter the awe-inspiring Mola Mola (or sunfish) and Manta but 100% will see Turtle.

The beach itself is relatively small, with golden sand and clear, calm waters ideal for swimming and relaxing. It's a popular spot for day trips from Bali due to its beauty and accessibility. The surrounding cliffs and lush greenery add to the picturesque setting, making it a favorite among photographers and nature enthusiasts. While the beach is relatively undeveloped, there are a few local eateries and facilities available for visitors.

The best way to experience Crystal Bay is by taking a boat tour or hiring a local guide who can show you the best snorkeling and diving spots in the area. If you walk to the left end of Crystal Bay, you'll find a narrow path that leads to another beautiful beach called Padang Beach . The walk along this path takes about 10 minutes and offers stunning views of the coastline and surrounding cliffs. Padang Beach is known for its soft white sand and clear blue waters, making it a great spot for swimming and sunbathing.
