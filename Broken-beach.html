Broken Beach, located in Nusa Penida, Indonesia, is a unique natural formation that has become a popular tourist destination. It is a natural bridge formation that arches over the ocean, creating a stunning circular cove with crystal-clear turquoise waters. The natural bridge is the result of erosion that has created a hole in the cliff, allowing seawater to flow into the cove.

The beach is surrounded by dramatic cliffs and offers breathtaking views of the ocean and the surrounding landscape. The waters around Broken Beach are known for their rich marine life, making it a popular spot for snorkeling and diving. Visitors can also enjoy the scenic beauty of the area by taking a leisurely stroll along the cliffs or simply relaxing on the beach and taking in the stunning views.

Access to Broken Beach is relatively easy, with a short walk from the parking area to the viewing platform. However, it is important to note that there are no facilities at Broken Beach, so visitors should come prepared with water, snacks, and any other essentials they may need. Overall, Broken Beach is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Nusa Penida, offering stunning natural beauty and a unique geological formation that is sure to leave a lasting impression.
