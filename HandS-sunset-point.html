H&S Sunset beach a very beautiful place to watch the sunset, not for the cowardly because after sunset you will drive for a long time in the dark, since there are no hotels, shops or houses nearby. During the day, in bright light, there is a stunning view of the rocky coast along the entire length possible for visibility.

Ahead there is a rocky ledge no worse than on Kelingking beach and the insane beauty of the turquoise light coastline.The only bad thing is that you can’t go down to the beach now and you can only see this beauty from a bird’s eye view. You can’t go down, because the stairs leading down are currently not suitable for going down. But on Nusa Penida there is no more beautiful view than here

Buy water and other drinks in advance; there is nothing around. The place is remote from other attractions; there are not a lot of tourists here.
